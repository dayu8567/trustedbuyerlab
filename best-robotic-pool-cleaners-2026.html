Do Not Overbuy

If your pool is small, flat-bottomed, and mostly collects light surface debris, you may not need the longest runtime or broadest cleaning-zone claim. A value model can be the smarter conversion choice.

When Premium Makes Sense

Pay up when you have a larger pool, frequent leaf load, visible wall or waterline grime, or you want fewer cleaning interruptions during a full pool cycle.
